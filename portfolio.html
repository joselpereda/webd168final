Project Highlights

Cloud architect for Cemex's worldwide application modernization initiative. Over 200 web and desktop applications were modernized and/or migrated to Azure and AWS. It helped the company increase its productivity 3x and slash IT capital costs by millions, as the company no longer procures computing hardware to host LoB applications. Furthermore, it simplified maintenance and monitoring of all their core IT services..

Latin American telcos were early adopters of Artificial Intelligence. Well before the arrival of ChatGPT and other LLMs, America Movil (largest telco in the region), embarked on an ambitious project to automate their extensive network of customer service call centers. The goal was to automate tier 1 calls, with the objective of decreasing customer wait times, resolution time, and by virtue, increase customer satisfaction. This is in fact what my team delivered.

Coppel is one of largest retailers in Mexico. Within their extensive network of stores, customers can purchase clothing, furniture, appliances, electronics, and can even do banking. The president and owner of the company decided to modernize their inventory management and POS by moving their corresponding backends to the cloud. Their chief objective was having platforms and tooling that could enable continuos and rapid innovation, all while keeping the cash registers ringing and customer deliveries going.
